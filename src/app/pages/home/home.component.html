Our Engagement Models

Our team is your partner for the next big digital leap. Our customized and highly flexible engagement models are designed to meet business objectives. All of our service engagements are focused on customer satisfaction, and we ensure that clear communication is maintained throughout.

Fixed Price
Model

We estimate the project after understanding its exact requirements and the projected development time. We share a fixed cost with our clients after outlining the exact time and deliverables.

Hourly Basis
Model

You can hire a professional on the number of working hours required for your project. With this engagement model, you have direct access to the expert you've hired and complete project control.

Dedicated Hiring
Model

Under this model, you only pay a fixed fee per resource you hire or use. It's perfect for some specific tasks where a group of developers works on specific software and oversees the project from start to finish.
